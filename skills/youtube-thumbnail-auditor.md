# YouTube Thumbnail Auditor

Score and improve YouTube thumbnails using a proven 20-point framework. Every audit is specific and actionable — never generic advice.

---

## THE CORE QUESTION — Before Anything

> **"What is this video going to show me, and do I want to see it?"**

If the answer takes more than 1 second — the thumbnail fails its primary job. Everything else is secondary to this.

---

## 20-POINT AUDIT CHECKLIST

Score each: ✅ Pass (full points) | ⚠️ Needs Work (half) | ❌ Fail (0)

### 🔴 CLARITY & PURPOSE (5 pts each = 25 max)

**C1. One Clear Promise**
- Thumbnail communicates ONE deliverable outcome
- Viewer knows exactly what they'll learn/see

**C2. 3-Second Rule**
- Viewer understands the video topic in 3 seconds at small size
- Test: squint at it — is the subject still identifiable?

**C3. Text — 3-5 Words Maximum**
- Bold, readable, 3-5 power words max
- Good: "Figma → Code FREE" / Bad: "In this tutorial we learn how to..."

**C4. One Clear Focal Point**
- Eye knows exactly where to look first
- No 3 equally-sized competing elements

**C5. Mobile Legibility**
- 70%+ of YouTube is mobile — must work at ~120px width
- Text, product, and subject all readable at small size

### 🟠 VISUAL QUALITY (4 pts each = 20 max)

**V1. High Contrast**
- Text vs background contrast is strong
- No white text on light backgrounds or dark on dark

**V2. Font Weight**
- Bold or Extra Bold only (Montserrat ExtraBold, Bebas Neue, Anton)
- Never thin, regular, or italic-only

**V3. Background Clarity**
- Background doesn't fight foreground elements
- Good: gradient, solid color, blurred image. Bad: busy screenshot as BG

**V4. Product/Subject Quality**
- If showing UI/product: high-resolution, cropped to relevant area
- No blurry screenshots or full-desktop views at tiny scale

**V5. Color Count**
- Maximum 3 colors in composition
- No rainbow gradients or 5+ competing colors

### 🟡 COMPOSITION (3 pts each = 15 max)

**D1. Visual Hierarchy**
- Clear reading order: Subject → Main Text → Secondary
- Size, color, position create flow

**D2. Text Placement**
- Text in safe zones: left 1/3 or center
- NOT bottom-right (covered by timestamp) or top-right (watermark)

**D3. YouTube Feed Contrast**
- YouTube UI is white/light grey — thumbnail doesn't disappear
- Avoid pure-white dominant thumbnails

**D4. Face Usage (if applicable)**
- If face: clear relevant expression (curiosity, surprise)
- If no face: product/output is the hero visual
- Face should never cover the product

**D5. Annotation Elements**
- Arrows, circles used deliberately (max 1)
- Not clip-art red circles from screenshots

### 🟢 CHANNEL CONSISTENCY (2 pts each = 6 max)

**E1. Recognizable Style**
- Subscriber could identify this as your channel before reading title
- Consistent font, colors, layout structure across videos

**E2. Series Visual Coding**
- Related videos look visually connected
- Consistent color/layout per series or topic

**E3. Appropriate for Niche**
- Educational: trust signals, show the output, competence
- Entertainment: emotion, faces, bold colors
- Review: product hero shot, comparison layout

## SCORING

| Category | Max Points |
|----------|-----------|
| Clarity & Purpose (C1-C5) | 25 |
| Visual Quality (V1-V5) | 20 |
| Composition (D1-D5) | 15 |
| Channel Consistency (E1-E3) | 6 |
| **TOTAL** | **66** |

**Grades:**
- 56-66: 🟢 **A** — Publish with confidence
- 45-55: 🟡 **B** — 1-2 tweaks before publishing
- 33-44: 🟠 **C** — Needs significant rework
- Below 33: 🔴 **D/F** — Rebuild recommended

## NICHE-SPECIFIC RULES

**Educational/Tutorial channels:**
- Show the OUTPUT, not the process
- Numbers work: "120+ Widgets", "5-Step Guide"
- "Before/After" compositions are strongest
- Don't use exaggerated shock faces — audience responds to competence

**Entertainment channels:**
- Faces with strong emotions drive CTR
- Bold colors, high saturation
- Curiosity gaps in text

**Review/Tech channels:**
- Product hero shot, clean background
- Comparison layouts (side by side)
- Stars/ratings as visual element

**Gaming channels:**
- In-game screenshot of the exciting moment
- Character art, bright colors
- Numbers (damage, scores, rankings)

## COMMON FAILURE MODES

| Failure | Why It Hurts | Quick Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Text over busy background | Unreadable on mobile | Add dark overlay behind text |
| 8+ words on thumbnail | Nobody reads in 0.5 seconds | Cut to 3-5 strongest words |
| Product screenshot too small | Can't see what video delivers | Crop and zoom in |
| Neutral color palette | Disappears in YouTube feed | Add high-contrast zone |
| Generic stock photo BG | Looks like every other channel | Use branded color or blurred real UI |
| Font too thin | Illegible on mobile | Use ExtraBold/Black weight only |
| Too many elements | Eye doesn't know where to look | Remove everything non-essential |
| White background | Invisible in YouTube's white UI | Add colored border or dark BG |
